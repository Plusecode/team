Barzig Lab Inc. is a Canadian software company focused on barcode technology, inventory systems, and warehouse workflows. We build AI-powered tools that give small and mid-size operations the barcode intelligence they need — without the enterprise price tag.

We started Barzig Lab because we saw a gap in the market: small warehouses and retail businesses needed professional barcode tools but were stuck choosing between consumer-grade apps that lacked standard barcode compliance or enterprise software that cost thousands and required implementation teams.

Our flagship product, VectorBarcode, is built to fill that gap. It runs entirely offline, generates industry-standard barcodes across all major formats, and exports print-ready files in SVG, PDF, PNG, and EPS. It's the tool we wished existed before we built it.

We are based in Toronto, Canada and committed to building honest, reliable software for the people who keep goods moving — warehouse managers, retail operators, small business owners, and logistics teams.

Our Values

Offline-first

Your barcode data never leaves your device. No cloud dependency, no network requirement, no data risk. We believe private data should stay private — always.

Standards-compliant

We take standard barcode compliance seriously. Real-time check digit validation, correct quiet zones, and accurate format specifications are non-negotiable in our products.

Right-sized

We don't build for Fortune 500 companies. We build for small warehouses, retail shops, and growing businesses that need professional tools without the complexity.

Honest pricing

Simple monthly pricing with no hidden fees. Pro is $2.99/month and you can cancel anytime. We believe software should be priced fairly.

Built to last

We write clean, maintainable code and commit to long-term support. Buying a Barzig Lab product means it will keep working years from now.

Customer-driven

Our roadmap is shaped by real feedback from warehouse managers and retail operators. If you have a feature request, we want to hear it.

Get in touch

We respond to every message within 2 business days. Whether it's a bug report, a feature request, or just a question about standard barcode compliance — we're happy to help.
