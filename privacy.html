Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Barzig Lab Inc. takes your privacy seriously. VectorBarcode is designed to work entirely on-device. Barcode generation and scanning are performed locally — no barcode data is uploaded to external servers.

Data we do not collect

  • Barcode values or data you enter
  • Scanned barcode contents
  • Generated images or exported files
  • Your History or Favorites lists
  • Camera images or video

Data stored on your device

VectorBarcode stores your barcode History, Favorites, notes, and app settings locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to Barzig Lab or any third party. You may delete it at any time from within the app.

Analytics

VectorBarcode does not include any third-party analytics SDKs. We do not track app usage, feature usage, or user behavior.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by the platform app store (Google Play). Barzig Lab does not receive or store payment card details. Please refer to the platform's privacy policy for details on payment data handling.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at plusecodeteam@barzig.com.
