Core Capabilities
Everything your warehouse needs to run smoothly

1D & 2D Barcode Generation

EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A, Code 39, Code 128, ITF-14, QR Code, DataMatrix & PDF417 — including GS1-standard retail formats — all in one app.

Scan & Regenerate

Use the built-in camera scanner to capture any barcode, then instantly regenerate it in a new format or color. Zero network calls.

Batch CSV Generation

Import a CSV and generate hundreds of barcodes in one step. Perfect for product launches, relabeling runs, and inventory resets.

Multi-Format Export

Export as vector SVG or EPS, plus PDF and PNG, at 300, 600 or 1200 DPI. Print-ready at any scale — from shelf labels to pallet sheets.

Offline & Private

All barcode generation and scanning happens on-device. No data is uploaded to any server. Ideal for secure warehouse environments.

History & Favorites

Every generated barcode is saved to History with full metadata. Pin barcodes to Favorites for quick re-export and print label workflows.
