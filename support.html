FAQ
Common questions
Does VectorBarcode require an internet connection?
No. All barcode generation and scanning is performed entirely on-device. No internet connection is required, and no barcode data is ever uploaded to any server.
Which barcode formats are supported?
VectorBarcode supports EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A, Code 39, Code 128, ITF-14, QR Code, DataMatrix, and PDF417.
What export formats are available?
Free users can export PNG at 300 DPI. Pro users unlock vector SVG and EPS, plus PDF, and PNG at 600 or 1200 DPI — all print-ready.
What is the difference between Free and Pro?
The free version lets you generate single barcodes and export PNG. Pro unlocks batch/CSV generation, unlimited history and favorites, SVG/EPS/PDF export, higher DPI PNG, ZIP batch export, and print label workflows.
Is the Pro upgrade a one-time purchase or a subscription?
Pro is $2.99/month. You can cancel anytime from your app store account.
Does VectorBarcode guarantee standard barcode compliance for production use?
VectorBarcode validates check digits in real time and generates barcodes according to published industry specifications. However, users are responsible for validating barcode compliance for their specific production environments. See our Terms of Use for details.
Is VectorBarcode affiliated with GS1 or Zebra?
No. VectorBarcode and Barzig Lab Inc. are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to GS1, Zebra Technologies, or any other standards body or hardware manufacturer. See our Disclosures page for full details.
Who owns the barcode data I generate?
You do. VectorBarcode is a rendering tool — it generates the barcode graphic based on the data you provide. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to use any barcode prefix or product number you encode. See our Disclosures page for full details.
What languages does the app support?
VectorBarcode is available in English, Français, Türkçe, Português, Indonesia, and Tiếng Việt.
